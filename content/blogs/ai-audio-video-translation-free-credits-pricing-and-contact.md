## What aantraa does

aantraa is built for **AI-powered audio and video translation**:

- **Video dubbing** — replace or add voice tracks in another language while keeping timing aligned with your footage.
- **Audio translation** — turn podcasts, voiceovers, and spoken content into new languages.
- **Multilingual output** — target many languages from a single source file.
- **Parallel jobs** — run multiple translation targets at once when you need scale.

Whether you’re localizing a product demo, a course, or social clips, aantraa focuses on **minutes of translated media** — you pay for what you use.
