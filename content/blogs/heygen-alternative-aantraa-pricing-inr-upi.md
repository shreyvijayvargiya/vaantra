## Pay in India: UPI, cards, and Dodo Payments

For Indian customers, checkout runs through **Dodo Payments**. You can pay in **INR** using:

- **UPI** (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other UPI apps)
- **Debit / credit cards**
- **Net banking** (where supported)

You see the total in **rupees before you pay** — no mental math from dollars. International customers can pay in **USD** on the same flow.

We chose this setup because many of our users are in India creating content for global audiences (and the other way around). Paying in INR with UPI is faster than hunting for a card that works on a foreign billing page.
